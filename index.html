Welcome to Sui-Gen

Sui-Gen is a precision lunisolar calendar generator powered by pre-computed astronomical data from NASA JPL's DE440/DE441 planetary ephemerides, calculated using Skyfield. New Moon timings and Solar Term crossings are resolved to sub-second precision for the full 619-17190 CE range. Each year is enriched with the zodiac animal, Wu Xing element, and Ganzhi designation, with full leap-month detection.

Astronomical Precision

Sui-Gen's astronomical data was pre-computed using Skyfield against NASA JPL's DE440 and DE441 planetary ephemerides, the same numerical integration models used for interplanetary spacecraft navigation. New moon moments and solar term crossings were resolved to sub-second precision and converted to Beijing Time (UTC+8).

Data Coverage

DE440 (covering 1550-2648 CE) was used where available, as it includes modelling of the Moon's liquid core and mantle coupling for higher accuracy. DE441 (covering 619-17190 CE) was used for the historical and far-future ranges outside DE440's coverage. Accuracy is sub-second within the DE440 range. For dates outside this range, accuracy degrades gradually due to uncertainty in ŒîT (the difference between Terrestrial Time and Universal Time).

Cycle Stability

Zodiac, element, and Ganzhi cycle fields are computed separately via pure 12- and 60-year cycle arithmetic and are 100% reliable for any year.
